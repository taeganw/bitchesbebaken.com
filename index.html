The craft

Fermented slow.
Baked with care.

Every loaf starts 24 hours before it hits your hands — wild yeast, time, and patience do the heavy lifting. No shortcuts, no preservatives.

The result: a shatteringly crisp crust, an open tangy crumb, and bread that actually stays good for days.
