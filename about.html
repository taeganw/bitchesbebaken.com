The story

About B;tches Be Bake'n

Who we are

Small batch. Big personality.

B;tches Be Bake'n is a sourdough cottage bakery based in Boise, Idaho. We bake everything in small batches — no shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromises.

Every loaf goes through a 24-hour cold ferment. Every pastry is made from scratch. We pop up at local markets, shops, and roadside stands around the Treasure Valley — and we ship.

We're not your typical bakery. We're a little edgy, a lot passionate, and absolutely obsessed with wild yeast.

The philosophy

Flour. Water. Salt.

Great sourdough doesn't need a long ingredient list. It needs good flour, clean water, wild yeast, time, and someone who actually cares about the outcome.

We source quality ingredients, stick to traditional techniques, and bake to order so nothing sits on a shelf. You get it fresh or you don't get it at all.

Find us out there

We go where the people are.

You'll find us at local pop-ups across the Boise area — roadside stands, boutique markets, community events. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram to see where we'll be next.

Can't make it to a pop-up? We do local meetup delivery around Boise and ship select items. Text us or fill out the order form to arrange.
