Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit an order request, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address (optional), pickup preferences, and order notes.

How We Use Information

We use your information to confirm and fulfill orders, coordinate pickup or delivery details, and respond to customer questions.

Form Processing and Bot Protection

Order form submissions are processed by Web3Forms. We also use Cloudflare Turnstile to reduce spam and automated abuse.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers needed to operate the website and process form submissions.

Data Retention

We retain submitted information only as long as needed for order fulfillment, customer support, and basic business recordkeeping.

Cottage Food Disclosure

Surfaces, utensils, and hands are cleaned thoroughly throughout the process. Breads, cookies, and pastries are made in a home kitchen and, under Idaho Cottage Food Law, are exempt from inspections. Products may contain allergens including wheat, dairy, eggs, and nuts.
